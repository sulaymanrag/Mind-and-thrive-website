About Mind and Thrive

Mind and Thrive is a mental health campaign led by six students at Fulton Science Academy in Alpharetta, GA. We’re driven by one mission: empower teens to build a healthy, balanced relationship with social media and their mental health. Through Instagram posts, educational workshops, community events, and fundraisers, we raise awareness about risk factors—like chronic stress, sleep disruption, and social comparison—and the protective factors that help teens thrive. We’re student-led, evidence-based, and committed to making a real impact.

Risk Factors & Protective Factors

Understanding both risks and protective factors helps teens build healthier habits. Here’s what we’re up against—and how to protect yourself.

Chronic Stress

Risk: Constant online engagement and pressure to respond can elevate stress levels over time.

Protective: Scheduled screen-free time and mindful boundaries reduce pressure and restore calm.

Sleep Disruption

Risk: Late-night scrolling and blue light exposure interfere with healthy sleep cycles.

Protective: A phone-free bedroom and screen curfew before bed support better rest.

Attention Fragmentation

Risk: Frequent notifications and multitasking reduce focus and academic performance.

Protective: Do Not Disturb during study time and single-tasking build concentration.

Increased Anxiety

Risk: Online validation cycles and constant comparison can heighten anxiety symptoms.

Protective: Real-world connections, hobbies, and self-compassion build inner confidence.

Social Comparison

Risk: Curated content can distort reality and impact self-esteem.

Protective: Knowing feeds are curated, valuing your own journey, and celebrating small wins build resilience.
